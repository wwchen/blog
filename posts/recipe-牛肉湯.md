牛肉湯
======

### Ingredients
  * 牛肉 (beef stew)
  * 罐頭蕃茄
  * 洋蔥
  * 紅蘿菠
  * potato

### Instructions
  1. stir fry beef with onions
  1. boil a pot of water; throw them in
  1. cook for a while; throw in potato chunks
  1. cook for a while more; throw in carrots
  1. throw in can tomato (and corn)
  1. add salt to taste
